Getting Started

To create a new route, go to the dashboard and use the Route Direction panel. Enter your start and end points, then click 'Find Route' to generate the optimal path.

All wildlife detections are displayed in the Recent Detections panel. You can filter and sort these detections using the available options in the panel.

Troubleshooting

Try refreshing your browser or clearing your cache. If the issue persists, ensure you have a stable internet connection and contact support if needed.

Ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you've forgotten your password, use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page to reset it.
