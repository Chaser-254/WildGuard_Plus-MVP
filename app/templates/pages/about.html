Protecting communities, Preserving Wildlife

WildGuard Plus is an AI Powered Early Warning System for Human-Wildlife Conflict in Taita Taveta County. It uses machine learning models to detect approaching wildlife especially elephants and it sends an instant alert to KWS for response coordination and community members for early action like evacuation.
